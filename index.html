Year In Review

For the last few years I've really wanted to track my "good" habits look at the data and see what I can learn from them. However, I would always burn out a few months into the process. This was the first year I was able to go a whole year tracking my habits. I used Daylio/Google fit to track my habits. Then imported the data into my own DB schema and plotted everything with ploty.

I tracked 6 habits that can be broken down into 3 categories.

Knowledge

  • Reading
  • Programming
  • Math Note: Lots of overlap with reading since I was reading books with math and was never quite sure how to categorize that activty. So this is just me with a pencial and paper figuring stuff out. It does not include watching math videos or reading math books
  • Does not include: Audio Books Reading - Educational videos (mostly math or ML)

Health

  • Running
  • Yoga
  • Weights
  • Does not include: Walks (2-3 for the dog) I did track this but it blows up the chart since I walk the dog almost every day
